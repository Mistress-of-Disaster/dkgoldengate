About Delly

A name behind every deal.

DK Goldengate was founded with a clear purpose: to give real estate developers access to the bridge and gap funding they needed — but couldn't find. Delband Kerachi saw a gap in the market and stepped in to fill it, not primarily to maximize returns, but to genuinely enable developers to get more deals done.

DK stands for Delband Kerachi. This is her business. Her name and her reputation are behind every project she funds. She partners with developers who understand that motivation alone is not enough — organization, disciplined budgeting, foresight, and the ability to pivot when challenges arise are what separate successful projects from costly ones.

DK Goldengate offers a transparent, personal approach that has become much too rare in private lending. What started as a commitment to second-position bridge lending has grown into a nationwide private lending operation offering both gap and full first-position funding — still boutique, still personal, still committed to every deal she takes on.
