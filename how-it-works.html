How it works

A straightforward process.
No surprises.

Two tracks — one for developers seeking funding, one for investors deploying capital. Both start with a real conversation.

Track 1

For real estate developers.

More on developer funding →
01
Quick call
It starts with a quick conversation. We get to know each other, you tell us about your project, and we assess whether it's a fit — project type, location, timeline, exit strategy, and entity structure. No commitment, no pressure.
02
Run the deal machine
If the initial call goes well, put your numbers to the test. Use the Deal Machine on our borrower hub to run your deal through our underwriting criteria. If it gets a greenlight, you're ready to submit.
03
Submit your package
A greenlight takes you straight into our onboarding app. Submit your complete package there — deal details, entity documents, financial statements, and experience profile. A complete submission moves faster.
04
Underwriting, closing & partnership
We review your submission carefully but efficiently. Once approved, we move to closing — in as little as two weeks. From there, we stay engaged. We're partners in this deal, not bystanders.
Basic requirements

To be considered for funding, projects must meet the following criteria.

  • Fix & flip or new construction of residential investment properties (single family to multifamily)
  • No commercial properties
  • Short-term bridge loan only — no conventional mortgages
  • No primary residences
  • Professional real estate investors or developers operating through an entity — no individuals
  • Preferred exit strategy: sale or BRRRR (refinance into DSCR)
  • Collateral preferred — developers who can bring assets to the table are given priority
Track 2

For investors.

More on investor track →
01
Start the conversation
Reach out to connect with Delband directly. We'll walk you through how the process works, what to expect, and what kinds of deals we typically bring to our investors.
02
Choose your deal
When a project comes through that matches your criteria, we share the full details — property, developer, plan, projected timeline, and exit strategy. You decide whether to invest. No pressure, no obligation.
03
Invest, earn, & get repaid
Your capital is deployed into the deal. We manage everything on the ground and keep you informed. At the close of the project — typically within twelve months — you receive your principal and return.
Minimum investment: $50,000 per deal
